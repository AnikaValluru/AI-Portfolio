ML · FORECASTING
⭐ Featured · Machine Learning

Forecasting Chocolate Sales

Applied four regression models — Linear Regression, RBF Network, Neural Network (MLP), and SVM — to predict chocolate sales revenue from 1,094 international transactions. The project went beyond basic modeling: comparing underfitting vs. overfitting across models, applying L1 regularization, 5-fold cross-validation, and early stopping to improve generalization. The Fast MLP Neural Network with early stopping achieved the best out-of-sample performance, while SVM delivered the most balanced generalization overall.
